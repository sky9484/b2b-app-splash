Executive Summary

Splash is a B2B cross-border payment engine built on Sui blockchain. Malaysian SMEs send MYR — recipients receive local currency — in under 5 minutes at 75–120 bps total cost. Phase 1 is one corridor (MY→PH), one liquidity rail (Hata Exchange only), one settlement layer (Sui + native USDC).

⚠ Phase 1 constraint: Hata Exchange is the only MYR liquidity rail available in Phase 1. Luno is deferred to Phase 2. Treasury sizing, SOR logic, and build plan all reflect this single-rail reality. Luno plugs in as Tier-3 in Phase 2 with zero Move contract changes.
The Problem
  • Malaysian SMEs pay 3–5% via bank SWIFT with 3–5 day settlement
  • Wise blocks businesses — RM 30k/day retail limit applies
  • Informal P2P is illegal under MSB Act 2011 — fines and freezes
  • No API-first, batch-capable solution for MY→SEA payroll
  • USD 8–12B Malaysian SME cross-border outbound annually, underserved
The Solution
  • B2B API-first engine: MYR in → PHP (or local currency) out
  • Sui blockchain settlement: sub-second finality, atomic PTBs
  • Enoki gas sponsorship: users never hold SUI or pay gas
  • Sumsub KYC + Elliptic KYT on every transaction
  • BNM MSB license track from Month 1
